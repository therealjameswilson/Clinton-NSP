Framework Translation

Reagan NSP Frame, Clinton NSP Setting

Reagan NSP preface

The Reagan preface identifies four thematic lanes for National Security Policy: defense policy and strategic planning; Soviet analysis and risk reduction; the strategic stockpile, machine tools, and export control; and national security and defense preparedness. This page keeps that skeleton but removes the Cold War center of gravity. For Clinton, Soviet analysis becomes post-Soviet and transnational risk reduction; stockpile and machine-tool issues become the broader technology, nonproliferation, space, and export-control lane.

Chapter Plan

Volume Chapters

The volume now opens with one chronological National Security Strategy chapter covering the full Clinton period, followed by a defense-strategy chapter and special issue chapters on National Missile Defense and the ABM Treaty, Technology Transfer, and Y2K Planning. Each chapter keeps the four NSP framework themes visible as cross-cutting tags rather than as the chapter sequence.

    Operating Rule

    Start with contiguous OA-box batches that map to chapter gaps, then use adviser and Records Management files as control layers. Oversight packets, final speeches, and public copies are useful only after the decision path is identified.

    On-Site Capture

    For each pulled folder, capture the folder front, withdrawal sheet, exact title, date span, routing marks, and any document-control cues before reading deeply. That keeps later FRUS selection review reproducible.
