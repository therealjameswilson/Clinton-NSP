Declassified Document Chronology

A stripped-down FRUS compiler packet for dated public and declassified records in the Clinton National Security Policy workspace. It is designed for source-note copying, CSV export, and quick chapter-level triage.

Skeptical-reader rule: summaries are finding aids. The date, source note, status label, source URL, and PDF link are kept beside each record so the documents, not the interface, carry the evidentiary weight.
